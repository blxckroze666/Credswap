How CredSwap Works

This is your personal time tracker. Nobody else sees this — it's just for you.

1
Log what you do for others. Every task — school runs, cooking, errands, childcare — goes in as time given to others.
2
Log what you do for yourself. Reading, creating, resting, a walk alone — that's time taken for you. Log it too.
3
Hit Find Time. The app looks at your patterns and shows you exactly where gaps exist so you can start protecting them.
4
This is yours. It lives on your phone. It saves automatically. Christine's community is where you share and celebrate — this is where you track.
