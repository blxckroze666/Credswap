How MomSwap Works

No money. No apps to pay for. Just moms in Christine's group helping each other.

1
Post what you can offer. Share when you're heading to the park or hosting a playdate and can take 1–3 extra kids with you.
2
Earn Time Credits. For every hour you watch kids, you earn 1 Time Credit — added to your wallet instantly.
3
Spend credits for your own time. Browse other moms' offers and use your credits to book a swap — giving you uninterrupted hours to create, rest, or just breathe.
4
Coordinate in Skool. Once a swap is claimed, DM each other inside Christine's group to confirm the details. This tool handles the credits — the community handles the trust.
